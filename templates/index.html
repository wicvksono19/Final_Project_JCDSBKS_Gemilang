BCCP GENERATOR

(Bank Customer Churn Prediction Generator)

This project is the final project of Purwadhika Digital Technology School Bekasi, as a Data Science and the purpose of this project is to find out the possibility that people will remain at the bank or will leave the bank. Solve this problem with various Machine Learning methods that can provide predictions that the person will stay or will leave the bank.
